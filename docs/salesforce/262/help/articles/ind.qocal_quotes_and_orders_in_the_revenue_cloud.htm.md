# Quotes and Orders in the Agentforce Revenue Management

Quotes serve as preliminary offers to propose products, services, and their pricing to customers. Sales representatives and partners create and revise multiple quotes before finalizing an order. Orders establish a formal agreement between the business and the customer by detailing the specific products, services, and pricing.

Agentforce Revenue Management provides tools for sales representatives, partners, and customers to manage these transactions efficiently. Both quotes and orders use shared functionalities, such as transaction management components and advanced pricing features.

Foundational Setup for Quotes and Orders
Execute these configuration procedures to optimize quote and order performance within Salesforce Agentforce Revenue Management. Prioritize the setup of the Transaction Line Editor, refine all pricing logic, and stabilize API invocation protocols to ensure seamless system integration.
Manage Quotes in Agentforce Revenue Management
Run these configuration procedures to optimize the quote management lifecycle and document generation within Agentforce Revenue Management. Prioritize the setup of quote line item imports and opportunity synchronization to accelerate record creation while maintaining data alignment across your sales pipeline. By stabilizing these foundational dataflows, you make sure that complex elements like bundles and attributes transition accurately from preliminary offers to finalized orders.
Manage Orders in Agentforce Revenue Management
Orders signify a formal agreement between your business and the customer, detailing the products, services, and pricing. Learn how to create and manage orders effectively.
View and Edit Quotes in Agentforce Revenue Management
Use the Transaction Line Editor or Sales Transaction Line Editor to add, configure, and price quotes in Agentforce Revenue Management. These editors provide a centralized workspace to manage product selections, apply discounts, and verify pricing details before final submission.
View and Edit Orders in Agentforce Revenue Management
The order page lists product and pricing details. Use this workspace to add, remove, and manage products within your transactions.
