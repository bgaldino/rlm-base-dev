# Considerations for Approvals in Slack

Keep these considerations in mind when working with approvals in Slack.

REQUIRED EDITIONS
Available in: Lightning Experience
Available in: Enterprise, Performance, Unlimited, and Developer Editions where Advanced Approvals or Flow Approval Processes is enabled
Slack notifications aren’t updated in real time when actions are taken in Salesforce or email. To see the latest status of the work item, open the record in Slack.
When an approval work item is completed, you can't review again regardless of the channel used.
Slack doesn’t show approval requests assigned to groups or queues.
Delegates don’t receive approval notifications in Slack.
