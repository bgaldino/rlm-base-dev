# Clone and Customize the Predefined Document Generation Templates

Clone and customize the predefined Document Generation templates according to your business requirements.

REQUIRED EDITIONS
Available in: Lightning Experience
Available in: View product and edition availability.
USER PERMISSIONS NEEDED
To clone and customize the predefined Document Generation templates:	

Collections and Recovery Admin

And

DocGen Designer

Collections and Recovery includes two predefined Document Generation Templates. Clone and customize these templates according to your business requirements.

FinalDebtRecoveryNoticeAndLegalWarning
OverdueAccountCollectionNotice
From the App Launcher, find and select Design Document Template.
Expand the OverdueAccountCollectionNotice template, and open the latest version.
From the action menu, click Clone.
Customize the template according to your business requirements, and save the changes.
Before you customize the cloned template, learn about tokens used in the template and token limitations.
Activate the template.
Repeat steps 1 through 5 for the FinalDebtRecoveryNoticeAndLegalWarning template.
