# Approvals in Slack

With Approvals in Slack, approvers can approve or reject requests and add comments without leaving Slack, while submitters receive real-time updates on their requests. Your Slack actions are saved to Salesforce, so approval data stays consistent across channels.

REQUIRED EDITIONS
Available in: Lightning Experience
Available in: Enterprise, Performance, Unlimited, and Developer Editions where Advanced Approvals or Flow Approval Processes is enabled

In Slack, reviewers can view approval-related details, such as the work item name, the related record, the submitter, and the condition that triggered the request. The approval and related records are visible in the Slack urfurl and flexpane components. To manage the fields in these components, see Slack Record Layout.

Actions performed in Slack are updated in Salesforce records. For example, approving or rejecting a request in Slack updates the approval work item's Status and Comments fields and the Action Channel Name fields on the related approval submission.
