# Supported Payment Methods for the Self-Service Billing Portal

You are here:

DOCS
AGENTFORCE REVENUE MANAGEMENT
Supported Payment Methods for the Self-Service Billing Portal

Give your customers more flexibility to pay invoices by adding digital wallets, bank debits, and buy now, pay later options to the self-service Billing Portal.

REQUIRED EDITIONS
Available in: Lightning Experience
Available in: Enterprise, Performance, Unlimited, and Developer Editions with the Revenue Cloud Billing license. Contact your Salesforce account executive for more information.

The payment methods available to your customers depend on the payment gateway that you configure.

Stripe

If you configure Stripe as your payment gateway, your customers can pay by using these payment methods.

ACH Debit
Affirm
Afterpay/Clearpay
Amazon Pay
Apple Pay
BACS Debit
BECS Debit
Bancontact
Credit Cards
EPS
Google Pay
iDEAL
Klarna
Link By Stripe
SEPA Debit
Adyen

If you configure Adyen as your payment gateway, your customers can pay by using these payment methods.

ACH Debit
Afterpay/Clearpay
Apple Pay
Credit Cards
EPS
Google Pay
iDEAL
SEPA Debit
