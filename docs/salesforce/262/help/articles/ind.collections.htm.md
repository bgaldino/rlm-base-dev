# Collections and Recovery

Collections and Recovery in Salesforce helps financial institutions and corporations streamline their collection processes, reduce delinquencies, and maintain positive customer relationships. The collections process is a critical aspect of financial management and operations for businesses, lenders, and financial institutions.

REQUIRED EDITIONS
Available in: Lightning Experience
Available in: View product and edition availability.

Collections and recovery refers to the process of pursuing customers to pay the money they owe to a business or financial institution. The various collection activities include sending reminders to customers, creating promises to pay, and encouraging customers to pay the money they owe as promised. This process also includes engaging with recovery agencies to reclaim money from delinquent accounts that have defaulted on payments.
